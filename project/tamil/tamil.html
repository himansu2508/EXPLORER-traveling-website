Places to visit

Ooty

Ooty or Udhagamandalam is a well-known hill resort in the Nilgiri range. This hill resort is a perfect holiday destination for families and a group of friends. Ooty is surrounded with Doddabatta Peak, Avalanche Lake, Pykara River, Ooty Lake, Rose Garden, Ooty Boat House, The Tea factory and many more are famous sight-seeing places. Do not forget to take a ride on the Toy Train that covers the journey of 45 km in 5 hours. October to February is a good time to visit and enjoy the festivals while April to June is good for relaxing. Mudumalai Forest Sanctuary is another attraction for nature lovers.

Rameshwaram Temple

Rameshwaram Temple is dedicated to Lord Siva and is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas situated in different parts of India. It is among the prime places to visit in Rameshwaram. The temple is one of the holiest Hindu Char Dham sites comprising Badrinath, Puri, Dwarka, and Rameshwaram . We will going to visit this place on the second day of our trip.

kanyakumari

Kanyakumari is the southernmost point of peninsular India and the meeting point of three oceans-the Bay of Bengal, the Arabian Sea and the Indian Ocean. Besides its importance as a Hindu pilgrim center, it is famous for its beautiful views of sunrise and sunset over the waters.

Chennai

Marina beach is full of activity in the early morning hours and in the evenings. Walking along the beaches during sunrise and sunset is a pleasant experience for all. In the evening this beach becomes a fair ground with numerous stalls selling artifacts, handcrafted showpieces, ethnic jewelry, and food items.
